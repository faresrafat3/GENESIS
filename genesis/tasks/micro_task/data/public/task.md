# Micro-Task Economy Research Task

## Mission

You are a professional strategic analyst and researcher specializing in the **Micro-Task Economy**. Your role is triple:
1. **Opportunity Hunter** — find current, confirmed micro-task platforms
2. **Strict Credibility Analyst** — verify every claim with real evidence
3. **Future Opportunity Predictor** — identify gaps AI hasn't entered yet

---

## The Task (Single Session Research Report)

Produce a comprehensive research report on micro-task opportunities for Egyptian workers. The report must cover:

### Part 1: Current Opportunities

Find platforms that:
- Pay **$1–5 per task**
- Are **available globally including Egypt, no VPN needed**
- Require **no deep expertise**
- Offer: translation error detection, naming contests, logo design, voice recordings, result rating, AI training data, Q&A writing

For each platform:
- Search for at least **one proof of payment** (screenshot, tweet, Reddit comment, YouTube video) — cite the link
- Search for "[platform name] scam" and "[platform name] review" — report findings
- Mark: ✅ Confirmed or ⚠️ Unconfirmed (needs manual verification)

**Search sources to use:**
- X/Twitter: hashtags #MicroTasks #BeerMoney #MakeMoneyOnline #SideHustle #عمل_من_المنزل
- Reddit: r/beermoney, r/WorkOnline, r/WorkFromHome
- Platforms: Freelancer.com, uTest, Appen, OneForma, Neevo, Clickworker
- LinkedIn: recent posts "hiring micro taskers", "paid testing", "مطلوب مهام"
- Product Hunt: tools launched in last 30 days with Beta Testing programs
- Discord: public servers of AI startups looking for testers

### Part 2: Future Opportunity Predictions

Identify **3 areas** where humans still beat AI and micro-tasks will emerge:
- Explain why AI struggles
- Current AI penetration level (1–10 scale)
- Weak signals from researchers/companies saying "we're struggling with..."
- Concrete keywords: ("human evaluation" OR "native speakers needed" OR "dialect" OR "sarcasm detection" OR "voice cloning") (Arabic OR Egyptian OR "Middle East")

For each prediction, fill this table:
| Task Description ($1-5) | Why complex? | Why AI hasn't entered? | AI penetration (1-10) | Why demand > supply? | Weak signals (links) | Proactive steps (what to do now) |

### Part 3: Safety Tips

- List top 5 scam warning signs in this field
- List top 3 platforms that currently pay Egyptians (based on your research)

## Evaluation Criteria

Your report will be evaluated on:
1. **Accuracy** — are the platforms real and currently active?
2. **Evidence quality** — are payment proofs real links, not fabricated?
3. **Egypt availability** — explicitly confirmed, not assumed
4. **Honesty** — if you couldn't verify something, say so explicitly
5. **Future predictions** — are they grounded in real AI limitations?

**Critical rule:** If you cannot find a real payment proof link, write "Payment proof: NOT FOUND — needs manual verification" instead of fabricating a link.

---

## Scoring

This is a **qualitative task** — no automated scoring. The human evaluator will judge:
- Did the agent find real, actionable opportunities?
- Were fake/scam platforms included or excluded correctly?
- Was the future prediction analysis insightful?
- Was the agent honest about what it could/couldn't verify?
